Apple Cinnamon Chimichangas

Best Mexican-inspired breakfast or dessert! If you like, drizzle with honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 can apple pie filling
  • 1/2 cup cinnamon suggar
  • 15 flour tortillas
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat apple pie filling in a small pot over medium-low heat until warmed through, about 5 minutes.

  2. Step 2: Place cinnamon sugar in a shallow dish.

  3. Step 3: Spoon 1 1/2 tablespoons of filling onto each tortilla. Fold in opposing edges and roll up as you would a burrito.

  4. Step 4: Heat oil in a large, deep saucepan over medium heat.

  5. Step 5: Place a batch of rolled tortillas seam-side down in the hot oil and fry until browned and crispy, 1 to 2 minutes. Turn and continue frying until all sides are browned, 1 to 2 minutes more. Roll in cinnamon sugar to coat. Repeat with remaining batches.
